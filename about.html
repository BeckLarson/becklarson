About Beck Larson

Currently working as a Java Developer at Linkware, Beck Larson is a Software Developer with a Bachlor's Degree in Computer Science of Game Design. He spends the vast majority of his free time focusing on freelance game design. While his specialty is in level design, Beck is skilled in almost all other aspects of game design. Beck has experiance with: Java, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, C#(with Unity), SQL, Jquery, TypeScript, REST API, Git, Trello, Unity, Blender, Affinity and more.
