Fall Of Light: Rise Of Darkness

FLRD was my first attempt at creating a visually pleasing game. The game was designed for my college class with the parameter idea of “Everything falls apart.” The players quickly learned that they must knock down structures to advance to areas. The creation of the game tested my scripting, designing, and building skills. It includes a full 2 levels and was created in Unity.

Adam's Eden

After the creation of F.L.R.D. I was inspired to create my first solo project. While this game is still in pre-alpha phase it has a number of aspects already. The player plays as a robot named Adam. Adam is sent to a newly discovered planet named Evo Prime to attempt colonization. Searching the sands could have been lonely so Adam’s creatures gave him a sentinel companion named Lilith. Adam and Lilith roam the sands as they attempt to unlock the secrets hidden benith.
