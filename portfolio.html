Evolution Zeta (Current Project)

Evolution Zeta is a third-person action MMORPG set in the futuristic galaxy of Zeta Reticuli. Players will have the option to choose from one of several races that inhabit the space station. By playing missions players skills will level up, allowing the to equip higher level weapons of the class that they currently have equipped. Each player has to option to control their own unique store which allows them to sell items to other players.

Habitable Planet and Moon

3d scene of a habitable planet and its icey moon. This served as great pratice for using a complex series of nodes within a 3d enviroment and my first time using Blenders Composer feature. The planet consists of three layers: Terrain, Atmosphere, and Clouds. The moon only has two layers as it lacks the cloud coverage.

Jagged Hero

Jagged Hero is a 3d, third person, role-playing game, where the player plays as a sheet of paper that has the ability to fold into various shapes resembling objects. The players main shape resembles a bipedal human-like shape as the character maneuvers throughtout various levels. As the player traverses the level they will obtain the ability to transform into different shapes, each giving a distinct advantage when encountering obstacles. On a basic level the player must sole sipme puzzels and collect tokens to unlock a final treasure room. The puzzles consist of pressure plates, moving platforms, and manipulation of character transformations to overcome the enviroment. Player transformation is useful for avoiding or completing objectives. Once the player collects all five Toy Tokens, they can enter the treasure room and complete the game.

The Sands of Eden

After the creation of F.L.R.D. I was inspired to create my first solo project. While this game is still in pre-alpha phase it has a number of aspects already. The player plays as a robot named Adam. Adam is sent to a newly discovered planet named Evo Prime to attempt colonization. Searching the sands could have been lonely so Adam’s creatures gave him a sentinel companion named Lilith. Adam and Lilith roam the sands as they attempt to unlock the secrets hidden benith. I quickly learned that it was impossible to make a proper game without custom assest, thus I was inspired to learn 3d art.

Fall of Light: Rise of Darkness

FLRD was my first attempt at creating a visually pleasing game. The game was designed for my college class with the parameter idea of “Everything falls apart.” The players quickly learned that they must knock down structures to advance to areas. The creation of the game tested my scripting, designing, and building skills. It includes a full 2 levels and was created in Unity.

Scrapper

Scrapper is a third-person, puzzle solving adventure game set in the distant apocalyptic future. While the player had no weapons, they could use a gravnul to pickup and move objects. This was the first semi-team project that I participated in during school. Each of us worked alone on oue own level, then worked togeather to combine them into one.

Gumball Funball

In college I was challenged to first recreate the famous Pong game. Then, expand upon it and turn it into a unique game that could stand independent from Pong. The result was Gumball Funball, a crazy mix between Pong, Soccer, and a maze. The game currently has 10 whole levels each with their own infuriating twist and challenge.
